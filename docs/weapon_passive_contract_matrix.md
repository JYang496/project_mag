# Weapon Passive Contract Matrix

This matrix is the implementation checklist for the unified passive contract. Combat HUDs consume only `charge_current`, `charge_max`, `ready`, `state`, and `progress`; detailed trigger and refresh rules stay here and in passive status text.

| Weapon | Trigger condition | Consumption | Refresh | HUD feedback | Required test case |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Auto Pistol | Periodic movement window opens, then Auto Pistol hits during that window mark targets. | Spend 1 of 3 charges when the window opens. | Reload restores all 3 charges. | Inner passive ring shows the periodic window/progress; 3 charge beans show remaining windows. | Contract test verifies 3 charges, window consumption, no new window at 0 charges, and reload refill. |
| Shotgun | Close-range shotgun hit triggers the close-hit passive; same-shot multi-pellet hits add the weapon-specific payoff. | Spend 1 of 3 charges per close-hit trigger. | Reload restores all 3 charges. | Ready inner ring while any charge remains; 3 charge beans decrement on triggers. | Contract test verifies 3 charges, repeated consumption to 0, reload refill, and offhand close-hit consumption. |
| Sniper | Far hit or marked-target hit triggers the distance/mark passive. | Spend 1 of 3 charges per valid hit. | Reload restores all 3 charges. | Ready inner ring while any charge remains; 3 charge beans show remaining shots. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 3-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Rocket Launcher | Kill with another enemy nearby triggers cluster payoff for grouped enemy clears. | Spend 1 of 3 charges per qualifying kill. | Reload restores all 3 charges. | Ready inner ring while any charge remains; 3 charge beans show remaining triggers. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 3-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Charged Blaster | One beam cast must hit multiple unique targets. | Spend 1 of 3 charges per qualifying multi-hit cast. | Reload restores all 3 charges. | Progress/ready inner ring for unique-target buildup; 3 charge beans show remaining casts. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 3-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Machine Gun | Spend magazine ammo, then start reload to trigger Heat Expansion. | Spend 1 of 1 charge when reload-start trigger fires. | Reload finished restores the single charge. | Inner ring shows spent-magazine progress; single bean empties until reload finishes. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Flamethrower | Accumulate heat, then start reload to gain Heat Prepared. | Spend 1 of 1 charge when reload-start trigger fires. | Reload finished restores the single charge. | Inner ring shows heat accumulation/progress; single bean empties until reload finishes. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Plasma Lance | Spend heat several times, then finish reload to trigger heat feedback. | Spend 1 of 1 charge on the reload-finished trigger. | Reload cycle restores readiness for the next chain. | Inner ring shows heat-spend chain progress; single bean shows readiness. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Glacier Projector | Freeze hit triggers Cold Snap. | Spend 1 of 1 charge per freeze-hit trigger. | Time recharge or reload restores the single charge. | Inner ring shows recharge progress; single bean refills on time or reload recovery. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Spear Launcher | Repeated same-spear damage builds Piercing Blade Dance charge; reload starts the radial volley. | Single HUD charge represents whether the stored blade-dance threshold is ready to spend. | Repeat projectile damage builds the next ready state; reload consumes the stored volley. | Inner ring shows stored charge progress; single bean means a volley threshold is ready. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Cannon | Stand still long enough, then fire the next breach shot. | Spend 1 of 1 charge when the breach shot fires; the shot gains direct damage, applies brief vulnerability to the direct target, and only keeps minor splash. | Reload restores the single charge. | Inner ring shows idle buildup/progress; single bean empties after the empowered shot. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Dash Blade | Dash far enough before the blade hit lands. | Spend 1 of 1 charge on the long-dash hit trigger. | Reload restores the single charge. | Inner ring shows dash distance progress while dashing; single bean shows readiness. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Chainsaw | Projectile wall contact triggers the wall-contact event; slowed-target hits still apply the vulnerability payoff. | Spend 1 of 1 charge on wall-contact trigger. | Reload restores the single charge. | Ready inner ring with a single bean until the event fires. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Orbit | Player taking damage triggers the orbit passive. | Spend 1 of 1 charge per player-damaged trigger. | Reload restores the single charge. | Ready inner ring with a single bean until damage trigger fires. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
| Laser | Laser beam hit triggers Focus Channel. | Spend 1 of 1 charge per beam-hit trigger. | Reload restores the single charge. | Ready inner ring with a single bean until the focus event fires. | Contract test verifies required status fields, 1-charge contract, non-empty trigger and refresh hints. |
